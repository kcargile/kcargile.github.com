It seems that yesterdays power outage has had little impact on PDC getting off to a good start. Other than a wait of over four hours to check in at the Wilshire Grand (which incidentally, isn't very), things kicked off pretty smoothly. Thanks to Michael Herman of Parallelspace for sharing his dinner table with me and for the great tips on getting the most from the conference.

In any event, gurus Don Box and Anders Hejlsberg gave an excellent demonstration of Avalon and Indigo during this mornings keynote, which included a visit from none other than, you guessed it, Napoleon Dynamite.
