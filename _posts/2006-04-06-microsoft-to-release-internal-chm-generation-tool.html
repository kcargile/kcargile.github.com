A (fairly) recent post by MS on the MSDN Product Feedback Center indicates that they may release their internal CHM/HTML Help generation tool to the general public. This seems to be in direct response to the community-wide perception that the NDoc project has been dead for over a year, however, NDoc admin Kevin Downs has posted around the net to the contrary. In fact, it is rumored that there is now a .NET 2.0-compatible alpha version of NDoc floating around out there.
