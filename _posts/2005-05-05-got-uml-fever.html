The UML is a very effective software design tool. It can help us stay agile, communicate with brevity, and prove concepts without hammering out a single line of code. Yes, UML is an effective tool—that is, when used by a qualified practitioner. Boeing's Alex Bell eloquently outlines just when UML might be your enemy in UML Fever: Diagnosis and Recovery (link compliments of ACM Queue).
