You two have probably noticed that I've been neglecting my blog for the past month or so. And despite what you might think, this is not totally due to laziness. I severely injured my shoulder—we'll say, rescuing a pack of newborn puppies from certain doom—and am just now getting my dexterity back.

The point I want to make here is nothing revolutionary. But by being sans-arm for 3+ weeks I got a very, very, very small glimpse at how difficult it must be to navigate software for folks who are disabled (and there's a lot of it out there that's difficult to use). So if you are developing, particularly for the Web/UI layer, take a moment to familiarize yourself with Section 508 and WCAG if you haven't already. ALL of your users will thank you for it.
