I blogged back in April about the possibility that Microsoft would release its internal document generation tool to the public. It appears that they have finally decided to indeed release 'Sandcastle' to the general public. And not a moment too soon...with the lack of progress in the NDoc camp and high expense of commercial products like DocumentX!, Sandcastle will likely be a welcome addition to more than a few .NET 2.0 developers' toolkits.
